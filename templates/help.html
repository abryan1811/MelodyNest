Help

See the FAQ's below

In order to upload music, go to the share tab and add the title, artist, select a genre and the instrument you played the piece with. Then upload a PDF of the sheet music you performed, upload an audio file of your performance of the piece and upload an image relating to your music (jpeg/png).

We don't yet have a reset password feature in place, but we will be working on that soon. Your profile won't be gone, but will be inaccessible for a while. You are welcome to set up a new profile while you wait. If you'd prefer we delete it, please send an email to admin@melodynest.com amd we will remove your profile for you.

Users must upload images, PDFs of sheet music and audios that they own or have permission to use. We at MelodyNest recommend using the google image tools to select images with permissions, or else we may have to remove them off the website. We also will not tolerate any offensive language or images. This includes but isn't limited to Racism, Gender, Homophobia, Pornography.

If you find any offensive content, or anything that doesn't follow the above policies has been added by a user, please send an email to admin@melodynest.
