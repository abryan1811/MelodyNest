Help

See the FAQ's below

In order to upload music, go to the share tab and add the title, artist, select a genre and the instrument you played the piece with. Then upload a PDF of the sheet music you performed, upload an audio file of your performance of the piece and upload an image relating to your music (jpeg/png).

We don't yet have a reset password feature in place, but we will be working on that soon. Your profile won't be gone, but will be inaccessible for a while. However, admin has the ability to change your password. Send an email to admin@melodynest.com stating your username and the admin will change your email as soon as they can.

Users must upload images, PDFs of sheet music and audios that they own or have permission to use. We at MelodyNest recommend using the google image tools to select images with permissions, or else we may have to remove them off the website. We also will not tolerate any offensive language or images. This includes but isn't limited to Racism, Gender, Homophobia, Pornography.

If you find any offensive content, or anything that doesn't follow the above policies has been added by a user, please send an email to admin@melodynest. The admin will be able to remove the content if deemed inappropriate, and send an email to inform the user.

If you can't find the answer you are looking for above, feel free to email admin@melodynest.com, and somebody will get back to you as soon as they are able.
